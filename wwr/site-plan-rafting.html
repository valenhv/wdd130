Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to let the audience buy online the tour packages and exclusive water rafting products.

Audience

The Target Audience is composed by middle class families and friend groups. Both have to be a minimum of 7 years old (accompanied by at least an adult).

What do they want?: They want to spend a fun and exciting summer outside.
What are their needs that aren't being met?: They need an affordable water rafting company that offers tour and recreational packages (planned activities). They also need to know about the company on the internet so they can comfortably choose what package they want.
How will my audience usually access my site?: They will usually access it both on desktop and mobile devices.
